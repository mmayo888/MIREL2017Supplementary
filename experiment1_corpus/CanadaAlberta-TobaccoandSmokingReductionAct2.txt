Section 8.4: Prohibition Order
(1) If a retailer is convicted of a 3rd or subsequent offence for a contravention of section 7.1 or 7.2, the Minister may by order in writing prohibit the retailer from selling tobacco products at the place at which the offence took place, or at any place to which the business of the retailer is moved, for the period of time provided for in the order.
(2) A retailer who fails to comply with an order made under subsection (1) is guilty of an offence and liable to a fine of not more than $100 000 for each day during which non compliance continues.
