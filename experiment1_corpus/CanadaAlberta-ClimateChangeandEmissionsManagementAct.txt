Section 45(1): Penalties
A person who is guilty of an offence under section 44(a) or (d) is liable
(a)        in the case of an individual, to a fine of not more than $100 000 or to imprisonment for a period of not more than 2 years or to both fine and imprisonment, or
(b)        in the case of a corporation, to a fine of not more than $1 000 000.
